I am a PhD student in the MATHEXP team at Inria Saclay under the supervision of Frédéric Chyzak and Pierre Lairez. My interests lie in computer algebra and experimental mathematics. My PhD thesis focuses on creative telescoping and computational D-module theory.

Software

  • MultivariateCreativeTelescoping
    • It is a Julia package to perform multivariate integration of D-finite functions using creative telescoping. This package comes with its own implementation of Weyl algebras and non-commutative Gröbner bases.
  • CreativeTelescoping
    • It is a Maple package to perform definite summation and integration of D-finite functions using creative telescoping.
