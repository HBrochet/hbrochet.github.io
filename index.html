Hadrien Brochet

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Quantum Field Theory group at the Max Planck Institute for Physics, working with Johannes Henn. Previously, I was a PhD student in the MATHEXP team at Inria Saclay, supervised by Frédéric Chyzak and Pierre Lairez. My research interests include computer algebra and experimental mathematics, with a particular emphasis on applications to the computation of Feynman integrals. My PhD thesis focused on creative telescoping and computational D-module theory.

Softwares

  • MultivariateCreativeTelescoping
    • It is a Julia package to perform multivariate integration of D-finite functions using creative telescoping. This package comes with its own implementation of Weyl algebras and non-commutative Gröbner bases.
  • CreativeTelescoping
    • It is a Maple package to perform definite summation and integration of D-finite functions using creative telescoping.
